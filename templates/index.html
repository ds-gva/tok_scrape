Welcome to the HFR Shitcoin TokenScraper (for BSC)

What would you like to do?

Check Tokens from last 10 minutes (test mode only - with auto-refresh every 1min)

Check tokens from last hour (with auto-refresh every 1min)

Check tokens from last 4 hours (with auto-refresh every 1min)

Check tokens from last 24 hours (can take up to 5 minutes - no auto-refresh)

How does the scraper work?

Pulls all the newly created token pairs on pancakeswap over te defined time period, then excludes tokens...

  • ...that do not have a verified contract on BSCScan
  • ...which have any of these three functions in their code: mint(), pause(), transfernewun() ; (all known as scammy, but can have legitimate use)
  • ...owned by their developer or with unknown owner (i.e., only retain dead addresses/renounced ownership and contracts as owners)

The scraper also provides helpful additional information and links to continue DYOR:

  • Owner type (contract or dead) and address, if contract
  • Top Holders (through BSCScan)
  • PooCoin to check charts
  • Pancakeswap LP v1 and v2 addresses (to check top holders
  • Time since token creation
  • Number of transactions in last 10min (in red, tokens with less than 10)

The scraper does not guarantee the robustness of the remaining contracts nor their performance -> DYOR and analyze carefully before investing!

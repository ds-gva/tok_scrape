How does the scraper work?

>Pulls all the newly created token pairs on pancakeswap over te defined time period, then excludes tokens...

  • ...that do not have a verified contract on BSCScan
  • ...which have any of these three functions in their code: mint(), pause(), transfernewun() ; (all known as scammy, but can have legitimate use)
  • ...owned by their developer or with unknown owner (i.e., only retain dead addresses/renounced ownership and contracts as owners)
  • ...with less than 10 transactions in the last 10 minutes

The scraper does not guarantee the robustness of the remaining contracts nor their performance -> DYOR and analyze carefully before investing!
